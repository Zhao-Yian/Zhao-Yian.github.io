Biography

Hi! I am Yian Zhao (Chinese name: 赵祎安), currently a Master student in Peking University (expected graduation in June 2026), advised by Prof. Jie Chen. Before this, I received a B.S. degree in Dalian University of Technology.

My research interests mainly focus on 2D/3D Visual Perception and Understanding , especially on Real-time Object Detection, Interactive Segmentation, and Interactive Manipulation and Stylization of 3D Gaussian Splatting.

Feel free to contact me by email if you are interested in discussing or collaborating with me.
